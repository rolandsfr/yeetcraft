About us

Yeetcraft is a kit pvp server with a map to fight several players at the same time and one for duels only. The map itself also provides parkour elements and has a lot of spots to encouter pvp fights. PLayers are getting coins for kills that they can later spend in in-game auction house. (More details in lobby)
