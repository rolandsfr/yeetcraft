About us

Yeetcraft is a minecraft kit pvp server with many different kits. There is a ranking system on the server which means you can get more and more powerful kits depending on how much you play. We have maps such as arena to fight many people at once and duels maps in case you want to fight someone in 1v1.

In order to get to know latest updates and news on the server, go to Updates page.
